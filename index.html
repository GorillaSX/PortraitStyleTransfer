Final Project: Style Transfer for Portraits

Jose Chavez and Daniel Li

cs194-26-adu, cs194-26-***

For our final project we take the paper "Style Transfer for Headshot Portraits" by Shih, Paris, Barnes, Freeman, and Durand, and implement it ourselves in Python3. A lot of the techniques used in the paper, such as Laplacian stacks, image warping, and matching, have been learned in this class. We then take our best implementation of the paper and experiment with its results on our different lighting scenarios.
